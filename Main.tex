    A continuacion, se muestra las ecuaciones deducidas del diagrama de bloques:
    
    \begin{equation}
        \frac{C(s)}{G(s)} = E(s)
    \end{equation}

    \begin{equation}
        E(s) = R(s)-B(s)
    \end{equation}

    \begin{equation}
        B(s) = H(s)C(s)
    \end{equation}

    Con estas ecuaciones, se puede deducir la función de transferencia en Lazo Cerrado:

    
    \begin{equation*}
        \begin{split}
            E(s)                &=  R(s)-H(s)C(s) \\[1.5ex]
            \frac{C(s)}{G(s)}   &=  R(s)-H(s)C(s) \\[1.5ex]
            C(s)                &=  G(s)[R(s)-H(s)C(s)] \\[1.5ex]
            C(s)                &=  G(s)R(s)-G(s)H(s)C(s) \\[1.5ex]
            C(s)+G(s)H(s)C(s)   &=  G(s)R(s) \\[1.5ex]
            C(s)[1+G(s)H(s)]    &=  G(s)R(s) \\[1.5ex]
            C(s)                &=  \frac{G(s)R(s)}{1+G(s)H(s)} \\[1.5ex]
            \frac{C(s)}{R(s)}   &=  \frac{G(s)}{1+G(s)H(s)}
        \end{split}
    \end{equation*}
